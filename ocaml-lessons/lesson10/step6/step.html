Modules to hide implementation

When you request a module with Number signature, it's important that any modules that have at least a compliant zero and add definition fulfill the requirement. You don't want to reject modules with additional functions and types.

That's why any module with at least the required definitions can by typed into a Number. Let's try:

module N2_raw = struct
  include N1
  let one = 1
end
module N2 : Number = N2_raw

Notice the difference between the signature of N2_raw and N2. Imagine you are given N2, how do you reach its one value?

Try to reach N2.one before proceeding to next() step.
